Problem Statement: Cookbook

Our users are those that have recently fallen on hard times financially. Most specifically, these users have come across financial discomfort due to COVID-19. Our solution looks to allow people to eat good quality meals on a low budget. The app will suggest low cost ingredients that can be used to make affordable meals of solid quality. The app allows for diversity of cuisine on as low budget therefor catering to many people during this pandemic. It is also very useful for people with little experience cooking as it provides any necessary ingredients as well as instructions on how to put the ingredients together into a proper meal.
